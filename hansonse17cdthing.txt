CDTHING - neccessary tables as estimated by Seth Hanson:

Artist Table
*Artist_ID
*Artist_Name
*Artist_Place-of-Origin
*Artist_Status (e.g. current, haitus, dead)

Album Table
*Album_ID
*Album_Title
*Artist_ID
*Album_Length
*Genre
*Release_Year
*Album_Cover-Art-File-Location

Song Table
*Song_ID
*Song_Name
*Song_Track-Number
*Song_Radio-Clean? (e.g. "Fuck the police" -> "N")
*Song_Length
*Song_Bitrate
*Song_File-Location
*Song_Comment (e.g. "I listened to this song on repeat while I climbed Mt. Everest")
*Song_Rating
*Song_Composer
*Song_Other-Contributors
*Album_ID
